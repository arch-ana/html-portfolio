Top Books of All Time According to Archana

My top 3 books of all time (Ordered alphabetically)

A Swim in a Pond in the Rain

A professor of Russian literature breaks down his favourite Russian short stories

Crying in H Mart

Musician and author, Michelle Zaunner, writes about her late mother in the context of grief, her identity as a half-Korean and the relationship they shared through food.

The Vegetarian

Nobel winner Han Kang writes about mental illness and a mentally ill person's relationship with the 'other'
